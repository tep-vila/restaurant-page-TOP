Adobo

Adobo is a classic Filipino dish made by marinating meat, usually chicken or pork, in a mixture of vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, and other spices. It's then slow-cooked until tender, resulting in a savory, tangy flavor with a rich, deep sauce. This beloved comfort food is a staple at family gatherings and showcases the unique blend of traditional Filipino cooking technique.
